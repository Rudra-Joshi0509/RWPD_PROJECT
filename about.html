About Symbiosis International University

Empowering Students Through Global Education

Symbiosis International University (SIU), established in 2002, is a multi-campus, multi-disciplinary university located in Pune, India. Recognized for its commitment to excellence in teaching, innovation, and international collaboration, SIU offers a diverse range of programs across Management, Engineering, Law, Design, and Liberal Arts. The University's mission is to nurture global citizens through quality education and a spirit of inclusiveness.
